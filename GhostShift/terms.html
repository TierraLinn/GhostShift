Terms Draft

GhostShift is a convenience automation tool for supported web players and visible user controls.

Platform limits

GhostShift does not guarantee ad skipping on every website, app, device, or platform. Support depends on browser capability, site layout, and platform policy.

Subscription

GhostShift is free to download and includes a 14-day trial. Continued Plus features require an active subscription after the trial.

Prohibited use

GhostShift must not be used to bypass DRM, paywalls, account requirements, platform security, or access controls.

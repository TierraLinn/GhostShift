GSGhostShift Dashboard

Privacy Policy Draft

GhostShift should collect only the account, subscription, settings, diagnostic, and support data needed to run the service.

What GhostShift should not collect

GhostShift should not collect video watch history, passwords, private messages, payment card numbers, or unrelated browsing history.

Payments

Subscription payments should be handled by Stripe Checkout. GhostShift should store Stripe customer and subscription identifiers, not card details.

Diagnostics

Anonymous diagnostics should be optional and controlled from the dashboard.

# GhostShift Store Listing Draft

## Short description

GhostShift helps reduce video interruptions where supported, with clear controls for skip assistance, muting, and cross-device settings.

## Full description

GhostShift is a freemium video-flow companion for people who want fewer interruptions while watching supported web video players.

GhostShift helps with:

- Supported browser skip controls
- Muting and restoring audio during detected ad breaks
- Cross-device settings
- Subscription and trial management
- Platform support guidance

GhostShift does not bypass paywalls, DRM, account requirements, or platform security. Availability depends on each browser, device, video service, and app store policy.

## Subscription

GhostShift is free to download. New users can try full supported features for 14 days. After the trial, continued Plus features require a subscription.

## Privacy notes

GhostShift should collect only the account, subscription, settings, and diagnostic data needed to run the service. It should not collect video watch history unless the user explicitly opts in for diagnostics or support.
